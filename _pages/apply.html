Program details

This program is designed for idea and early-stage startups. The SMART Visa is given to startups in special industries or with demonstrated growth potential. Please carefully review the criteria below to determine if you and your startup idea are a match for this program.

    Founders applying to this program must be:
  1. Prepared to actively work on their startup idea for the duration of the 6-month program
  2. Able to demonstrate relevant training and expertise in the subject area of the business
  3. Hold a passport valid for more than 6 months from the program start date in September 2020
  4. Acquire health insurance for the duration of the program
  5. Acquire health insurance for the duration of the program

    Price & Payment Terms
