Live and Work in Chiang Mai while you build your new startup

September 2020 - February 2021

The SMART Startup Bootcamp is a 6-month program during which you are invited to live in sunny, beautiful Thailand as you develop your next big idea. Backed by regional and global partners, not only do you get the SMART Visa, allowing you to legally live and work in the Kingdom of Thailand, through our network you will also receive resources, mentorship, and fundraising assistance to take your business to the next level. Discover why Chiang Mai is an ideal place to develop your young startup. Those wishing to register a company in Thailand will also receive specialized assistance.

Program Requirements

This program is designed for idea and early-stage startups. The SMART Visa is given to startups in special industries or with demonstrated growth potential. Please carefully review the criteria below to determine if you and your startup idea are a match for this program.

    Founders applying to this program must be:
  1. Prepared to actively work on their startup idea for the duration of the 6-month program
  2. Able to demonstrate relevant training and expertise in the subject area of the business
  3. Hold a passport valid for more than 6 months from the program start date in September 2020
  4. Acquire health insurance for the duration of the program
  5. A clean bill of health and medical certificate issued no more than X days before flight if entering Thailand from abroad.
    Startup idea must be within the following industries and niches as specificed by the SMART visa program:
  • Affluent, Medical & Wellness Tourism
  • Agriculture & Biotechnology

  • Digital Platforms & Solutions

  • Food for the Future
  • Human Resource Development in Science & Technology
  • Medical Hub

Program Details

The SMART Startup Bootcamp is designed as an accelerator-style program where founders are expected to work independently and manage their own time. This is a fee-based program, and no equity will be taken.

Content & Format

This bootcamp program will provide founders with the fundamental knowledge and skills to develop their startup idea. No prior background knowledge is assumed and this program is suitable for both technical and non-technical founders. New founders will benefit from a well-rounded introduction to all things startup while founders with prior experience will get value from the mentorship and networking portions of the program. As we expect founders will have differing levels of knowledge in various subject areas, participants are expected to take charge of their own learning experience.

    Program content will cover the following subject areas:
  • Business Planning
  • Go-to-Market Strategy
  • Product Development
  • Accounting and Finance
  • Management & Soft Skills
  • Branding, Sales & Marketing
  • Pitching/Public Speaking
  • Investment Fundraising

Program materials will be made available online with supplementary study groups, coworking sessions and mentoring provided on an ad hoc basis both online and offline. The majority of program content will be self-paced, but specific workshops and seminars will also be delivered in a live, interactive format. Special workshops will be held to cover specific subject areas and program managers can facilitate additional workshops and training at the request of program participants.

    Special workshops will be help on the following topics:
  • Business Registration and Company Incorporation in Thailand
  • Applying for the long-term SMART Visa (2-4 years)
  • Thailand BOI Incentives and Privileges for Foreign Startups

Three events will anchor the program, providing founders with greater access to talent, opportunities and investment in Chiang Mai, in Thailand, and beyond. These three events offer some of the best value from this program.

  1. Orientation & Ecosystem Tour | September X - X, 2020: Welcoming participants to the city of Chiang Mai, this two-day orientation introduces founders to the program, taking them to some of the city’s key startup sites and facilitating connections and introductions that will help them to make the most of this program.
  2. Matchmaking Event | December X, 2020: A curated networking event, this will be a highlight of the program. It is at this event that we will facilitate introductions to the greater Chiang Mai community. Held three-months into the program, this gives us a chance to understand who our startup founders need to meet. Some may be looking for technical co-founders or manufacturers while others may need to hire a marketing agency or sales-people. Whatever your people needs Matchmaking day is when connections will happen.
  3. Demo Day | February, 2020: Time to shine! A chance for the public to learn about the startups in the cohort and an opportunity for a select number of startups to publicly pitch in front of investors.

Additional dinners, nights out, coworking sessions, masterminds and other events will also be organized on an ad hoc or informal basis to help founders connect and support one another throughout the program.

Fundraising Assistance

The SMART Startup Bootcamp is intended to help startups validate and develop their ideas, with the goal of becoming investment-ready by the end of the six months. With this in mind, fundraising assistance will be provided to startups via Draper Startup House Ventures, a global ventures syndicate that helps startups match with funding. DSH Ventures works with venture capital funds as well as business angels, incubators and private investors. Funding partners on the platform include billionaire Tim Draper’s network of 23 global funds and prominent VCs from Asia Pacific and around the globe.
